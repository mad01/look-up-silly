Look Up, Silly! - Break the Scroll Habit with Mindful Challenges

Take back control of your screen time. Look Up, Silly! helps you build healthier phone habits by adding a mindful pause before opening distracting apps.

--- HOW IT WORKS ---

Choose which apps to block. Before you can open them, complete a quick mental challenge. This simple friction creates a moment of awareness, helping you decide if you really need that app right now.

It's not about restriction—it's about intention.


--- FIVE UNIQUE CHALLENGES ---

• Path Recall - Remember and trace a pattern
• Color Tap - Match colors, not words (Stroop effect)
• Quick Math - Solve simple arithmetic
• Tic-Tac-Toe - Beat the AI opponent
• Micro 2048 - Complete a mini puzzle

Each challenge takes just seconds but creates the pause you need.


--- POWERFUL FEATURES ---

• Block any app using Screen Time API
• Set "Always Allowed" exceptions
• Pause challenges when needed
• Schedule blocking times
• Track your challenge statistics
• View completion streaks
• Customize unlock duration
• Works completely offline
